**Theorem (Above Threshold Not Factorion).** For every natural number $n$, if $\dfrac{n}{\ln n} > 362880$, then $n$ is not a factorion. Here $362880 = 9!$, and a *factorion* is a natural number equal to the sum of the factorials of its decimal digits.
